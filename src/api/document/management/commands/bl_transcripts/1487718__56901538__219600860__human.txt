Thursday 3d April 1729
At a Joynt Comittee for Correspondence and Factorys
Present
The Sub: Govr. Sr. Rd Hopkins
Mr Ashton Mr Brooke
Mr Bowles Dr. Wood
Capt. Jones
The Comittee Settled the private Instructions to the
Commanders of the Buenos Ayres Ships
Adjournd
Tuesday 15 April 1729
Ata Joynt Comittee for Correspondence and Factorys
Present
The Sub: Govr: Mr Ashton
Mr Bowles Dr. Wood
Mr Roberts Mr Brooke
Mr Burrell Mr Halsey
Capt. Jones
The Panama Factory having perused the Papers in the
Secretarys Custody which were reced by Mr Hutchinson from
Jamaica and by him deliverd into the Company, and acquainting
the Comittee that they apprehend they or some of them would be
of use in giving them light into the Transactions of the late
Factory
Orderd That they be deliverd to Messrs Rigby &c, and that
true Copys attisted be left in the Companys Possession: And Mr
Hutchinson
