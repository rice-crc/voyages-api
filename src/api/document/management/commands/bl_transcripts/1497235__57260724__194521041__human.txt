22 In case it should happen that none of the Companys Sloops
employ'd between Jamaica and the Factorys, Should be at Jamaica
when the Negroe Ships arrive there, and when Negroes may be
much wanted at one or other of the Factorys, We think fit to direct
that if the Company's Agents at Jamaica Shall send you any
Negroes in a hired Ship or Vessel (which We have given them
Power to do) that you on your parts comply with such Conditions
as shall be stipulated in the Charter Partys
23 If it should happen that either of you should dye (which
Godforbed) We direct that the Survivor or Survivors Send us advice thereof
by the Very first opportunity and take Care to get in and preserve the
Estate of the Deceas'd, keeping an Account thereof in the Company's
books, transmitting the same and an Inventory to the Court of Director
for the time being, as also to the Relations of the Deceasd and Sendin
to our Agents at Jamaica to be forwarded home, the produce of the
Deceasds Estate by the first of the Companys Sloops, and should
either of you happen to dyes Mr Hutchinson whom we have appointed
your Book keeper is to succeed as Janr Factor
