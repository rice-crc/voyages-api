Tuesday 23d Feby 1730
At a Comittee for Correspondence & Factorys
Present
The Depy Governor
Mr Roberts
Mr Savill
The Comrs. prepared a Letter to the Havana Factory
Also a Letter to the Panama & Portobelo Factory,
to go by Mrs Hamart, and another to Mr Consul
Cayley of Cadiz, and Orderd them to be laid before the Court
Adjournd
Thursday 23th Febry 1730
At a Comittee for Correspondence and Factorys
Present
The Sub Govr The Depty Govr
Mr Roberts Sr. Jno Lock
The Comittee prepared Letters Vizt
Two to Messrs Pratter & Rigby One recomending Mrs
Hamart & to be delivered her under a flying Seal, And the other
enclosing the Dispatches for the Havana Factory
Also prepared an Answer to a Letter recd from Mr
Wright
