Tuesday 1t. May 1731 Tuesday 11th. May 1731
At a Comittee for Correspondence & Factorys At a Comittee for Correspondence & Factorys
Present Present
The Depty. Govr. The Depty. Govr.
Sr. Jno Lock Mr Savill Sr. Jno Lock Mr Savill
A Letter prepard to Mr Stratford in Arisur to his of the 30th of
Murch last, was read and Agreed to And Order'd to be Sign'd by the
Sevry as by Order of Court
The Comittee gave directions to Mr Read with Respect to
his forming a State of the Accots unadjusted by the former Agents at
Cadiz in Order to its being sent to Mr Consul Cayley
Also to the Sevry to prepare a Letter to Mr Pitt the Comp
late Agent at Cadiz to draw out & transmit to Mr Consul Cayley the
Comps. Accot Currt and such other Papers & Accot as he has in his
Custody belonging to their Depundencys
Adjourn'd
