Thursday 15th March 1730 Thursday 18th March 1730
At a Comittee of Correspondence At a Comittee of Correspondence
Present Present
The Depty Govr Mr Roberts The Depty Govr Mr Roberts
Sr. Jno Lock Sr. Jno Lock
The Comittee prepard a Letter to Mr Kene to go by this Nights Past
and Ordr the same to be Sign'd by the Secry as by Order of Court
Mr Kene having sent to the Comps One Orige Cedula for
the Restitution of the Comps Effects reprisals Anno 1718 & still
remaining unrestord; The Coms are of Opinion that it be imediately
forwarded to the Panama Factory as a place of greater Consequence
in this Respect than any other Factory.
Adjourn'd
