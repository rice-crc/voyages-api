Tuesday 20th June 1732.
Al a Comittee for Correspondence & Factorys
Present
The Sub Governor
The Deputy Governor
Sir John Lock Mr Burrill
Mr Roberts Mr Savill
The Comittee made some progress in Settling the Instructions for the
Comander of the Assiento bound for Buenos Ayres as the Comps Store
Ship pursuant to the 34t Article of the Assiento Contract, and
deferd the further Consideration to next Meeting.
Adjourn'd
Thursday 22d June 1732.
Al a Comittee for Correspondence & Factorys
Present
The Sub Governor
The Deputy Governor
Sir John Lock Mr Burrill
Mr Roberts Mr Savill
Mr Bowles
The Comittee took into Consideration the Courts Reference of the
26th Ulto concerning the Obliging the late Factorys at Buenos
Ayres to return home by the first Ships that shall be Dispatchd
