June 1 1720
To M^r. Pytts
S^r. I rec^d the favour of Yours & return you my humble
thanks for y^r oblinging complyance with my request in
behalf of S^r. Rob. Raymond as also for the Intelligence you
were pleas'd to send me relating to the Baron of Burfords
Estate, which I am told is bought by One M^r. Bowles a
Glassman after the rate of 37 years purchase, a price
abundantly too high for me. I hope you will not think
me unmindfull of the concern you recomended to me
in the effecting of which no endeavours shall be wanting
on my part & I hope a little time will show you that
I have been successfull in it. I think it is half a year
that is due to you.
I am &
Marg. de Montalcon
