Wednesday 26th. November 1718
At a Comittee of Correspondence
Present
Mr Astell
Sr. Wm Chapman
Mr Blunt
Mr Chester
The Comittee Pursuant to Order of the Court this day wrote a Letter
to Mr Pitt of Cadiz in answer to his of 17th Ulto
Adjd.
Friday 28th November 1718
At a Comittee of Correspondence
Present
The Depty Governour
Sr. Wm Chapman
Mr. Gore
Mr. Chester
Sr. John Lambert
Mr. Fellowes
The Comittee Examined Mr. Bedell upon the State of the Company's
affairs in New Spain, and gave him Direction to draw it up in writing with such
Vouchers and Papers as are necessary to prove the same
Adjd.
