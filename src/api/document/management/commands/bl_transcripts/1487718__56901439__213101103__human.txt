Wednesday 15 May 1728
At a Comittee of Correspondence and Factorys
Present
The Sub: Govr
The Dept Govr.
Mr Burrell
Mr Brooke
Mr Bowles
Sr Rd Hophins
A Letter prepared to Mr Keen in relation to the defects
Apprehended in the Orders sent to the Vice Roys of New Spai and
perve for Restitution of the Companys Ships Dessells, and Effects,
was read and agreed to; And the Secretary was directed to draw up
a Postscript to be added thereto directing Mr Keen immediately to
to apply for a Restitution of the Companys Sloop Du Lewis, Seized
at Havana, which it is reported has been long Since arrived at Cader
and the Snuff which was brought to Old Spain by the Spanish Man
of War calld the Sd. Francisco Zavier, lately arrived at Port Passage
from Havana and for any other of the Companys Ships, Vessells or
Effects, which upon Enquiry he finds may have been brought to
Old Spain
Read in Court
the 17 Do
an Agred to WS
Adjournd
