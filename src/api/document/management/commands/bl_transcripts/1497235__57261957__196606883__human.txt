Messrs. Rigby & Pratter
Gentlemen
The Court of Dirctors wrote you the 26th ulto
by the Cartagena Factory who are Embarking for Jamaica on
board the Neptune Capt. Winter inrelation to your furnishing
them with Negroes to take with them to said place;
They also wroke you under the same date, by the Gentlemen
of the Havana Factory who will deliver you this, for your
furnishing them with Negroes for their Factory.
I am now orderd by the Court of Directors (Mr Wescomb
being Indisposd) to acquaint you, that they have recd. your Letters
advising of the death of Mr Rigby (who was Chief of ye Panama
& Portobelo Factory) For the loss of whom They are very much
concern'd, and that they not being come to any Determination
touching said Factory, They intend to write you fully P the next Ship
I am
Gentlemen
23 Octr 1729
Yor most humble servant
W Smith
