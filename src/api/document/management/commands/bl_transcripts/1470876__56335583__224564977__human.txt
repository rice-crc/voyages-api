Wednedday 31st July 1734
At a Comittee of Correspondence
Present
The Sub Governor
Sir John Lade Mr Cowslad
Mr Gaultier
The Comittee read and approvd of a letter prepared
to His Excellency Mr Keene, and order'd it to be
Copied fair and laid before the next Court
Also made a progress in the other Dispatches
prepared for the Agents at Barbados, and agreed to
proceed further to morrow
Read, and Letter sign'd
2d August
Adjourn'd
