43.- We recommend it to you to methodize your Correspondence
for our more easy turning to particular Subjects by writing under
heads in the following manner vizt
1st Head Concerning Letters writ & Letters received
2. Concerning the Effects of the Company Recovered
3. Concerning Negroes their Receipt Measurement
and Sale
4 Concerning Seizures, Confiscations, Iudultos and
Illicit Trade & Cedulas & processes relating thereto
5 Concerning other Cedulas
6 Concerning Shiping
7 Concerning Judges Conservators & other Spanh officers
8 Concerning Goods & Merchandize
9 Concerning Trade in general
10 Concerning Accounts
11 Concerning the State of the Factory
And under any other remarkable Heads of Business
that may occur.

you have dispatchd away the originals, and lest any of your Letter
should miscarry and not come to our hands in due time, You are to fi
