It is the opinion of this Committee that the said Request
be granted, and that directions be given to the Agents at
Jamaica accordingly
The Comittee read the Letters receivd from the said Agents
and orderd the Secretary to prepare an answer to them
The said Agents having advised that they have but
few Passes left
Ordered
That the Court be moved to Empower the
Committee of Treasury to affix the Companys Seal to 24
Passes to be sent with the neat Letter
Read in Court 18 do
And agred to
Adjourn'd
