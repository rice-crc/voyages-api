The Committee prepard Letters to his Excs Mr Stanhope
and Mr Stratford to go by this Nights past and orderd them to be
Sent to the Directors Houses to be Sign'd by a Majority of the
Court.
The Comittee also agreed to Instructions to be given
to Mr Innes going Surgeon to the Panama and Ports belo Factory
in the room of Mr Gibson decad, And to an Instrument to pass under
the Companys Seal deputing him in that Capacity and to a Letter
to the said Factory hereupon
Also agred to the form of a Power to be given Messrs Herne
and Pitt of Cadiz, and in the Absence of the first to Mr Hugh Hancock
to Act in the Sale of the Watkinsons Cargo now arrived there, and
of Such future Cargoes as may arrive for the Companys Account
Read in Court
the and aageed to
Adjourn'd
