6 Being got to Havana you are forthwith to signify
your Arrival to the Governor and to wait on him with our Letter
herewith delivered you, and make him our Compliments presenting
to him at the same time your power under the Companys Seel which
is in the usual form) and We hope he will without any Seruple of
Embarrasment admit you as the Companys Factors and Register your
powers accordingly of necessary
7 You are in the like manner to wait on the Royal Officers
and make them our Compliments delivering to them our Letter also
herewith delivered you
8. And in as much as it is highly necessary for the better
