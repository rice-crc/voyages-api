Nednesday 19 Feb. 1728 Wednesday 19th Feby. 1728
At a Joynt Comittee for Corespondence and Factorys At a Joynt Comittee for Corespondence and Factorys
Present Present
The Sub: Gov. The Sub: Gov.
Mr Ashton Mr Ashton
Mr Brooke Mr Brooke
The Comittee taking into Consideration that it will be
necessary to have two Writers to be Employd in the Porto belo and
Panama Factory, and the Factors being obligd by their Articles to
maintain Two, and Mr Peter Sergeant being recomended to the
Comittee as a very dilligent Person, and one that writes a very
good hand, understands Spanish and French, and has made
a Considerable progress in Accot. The Comittee are therefore of
opinion to recomend him as a Writer to the Portobelo and
Panama Factory to be added to Mr Davison the other Wiiter
The Comittee read over the Draft of the Covenants between
the Company and the Portobello and Panama Factory, and made
Some Amendments thereto
Adjournd
