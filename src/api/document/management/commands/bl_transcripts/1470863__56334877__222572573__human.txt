Tuesday 23d January 1732/3.
At a Comittee for Correspondence & Factorys
Present
The Sub Governor
Mr Burrell Mr Savill
Mr Roberts Sr. John Lock
The Comittee having gone through the Letter prepared to
the Panama and Portobelo Factory, and Settled the same directed
it to be Copied out Fair and laid before the Court
Resolv'd
That this Comittee will meet again on Thursday
the 25th Instant
Adjourn'd.
Friday 26th January 1732/3
At a Committee for Correspondence & Factorys
Present
The Sub Governor
Mr Roberts
The Comittee went thro the Letter prepared to the Carthagena
Factory, and Settled the same, and orderd it to be laid before the
Court
Adjourn'd.
