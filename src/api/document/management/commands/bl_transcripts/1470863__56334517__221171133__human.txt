Tuesday 29t June 1730 Tuesday 29: June 1730
At a Comittee for Correspondence and Factorys At a Comittee for Correspondence and Factorys
Present Present
The Sub Govern. The Dep: Ger The Sub Governr. The Dep: Govr
Mr Rigby Mr Roberts
The Comittee prepard Letters to the Govrs: and Royal
Officers of Havana and St. Jago de Cuba recomending the
Companys Factors, and Concerns in those parts to their
protection.
Also prepard Lettert to Messrs Denniss Tassell and
Cock, acquainting them of the present disposition with regard
to the Two Factorys on the Island of Cuba, and are of Opinion
that a duplicate be Sent to Each of the four Factors.
Read in Court
3d July 1730
and agreed to
and 8 lres sign'd WS
Adjournd
