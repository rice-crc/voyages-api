Sir,
The Assients being again Stablished by the
Preliminary Articles & other Acts which have been long since Circulated to
the several Governments in the Spanish America, and the general Tranqui
lity being sinc Confinned, and a good Harmony restored between p Powers
of Europe, particularly those of Great Britain and Spain, We take
this first opportunity of Congratulating with your Lordship thereupon
hoping that no fucture Interrupton will anse to ouer Comore, but that
under the Sanction of the Treatys We may carry on the same free from
the Imtarrasments it has hitherto met with, to the mutual Advantage
of both Crowns as well as the Company
In Consequence which We have againsetled out
Factory at Your place under the Management of Mr Edward Garthwaite
and Mr Thomas Nasmyth, who will have the honour to deliver this
to Your Lordship; and being sensible how much it imports o general
Interest of the Assients That our Factors and Concerns should have your
Countenance & Protection Heeamestly recomend them to your favour
not doubting that from your Lordships Justice and other good Qualitie
inherent in you We shall experience an imediate Respitution of our
Effects Imbargd & a luccessful progress in our future Comerce
For our parts Weshall be glad of any opportunities of
Convincing Your Lordship of our readiness to you the most acceptable
Senvrces in our power being with singular Esteem
Yor Lordships
Most Obedient humble Servants
The Court of Directors of
the Roy Asseents Company
