Thursday 6th March 1717. Morning.
At a Comittee of Correspondence
Present
The Deputy Governour
Mr Astell
Mr Fellowes
Mr Chester
Sr. John Lambert
The Committee made a Progress in preparing a General Letter
to the Buenos Ayres Factory and will proceed further in the Evening
Adjd.
Thursday 6th March 1717 Evening
At a Comittee of Conespondence
Present
The Deputy Governorr
Mr Gore
Sr. John Lambert
The Comte wrote Letters to Coll. Stanhape and to go P tonights post
They also made a further Progress in the General Letter to the Buenos
Ayres Factory.
Adjd.
