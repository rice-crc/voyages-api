The Secretary acquainted the Comittee, that in the Letter
to the Agents at Jamaica approvd of the 20 Inst, mention
is made of 50 Passes to be sent therewith, but that he finds
only one is left and having desired directions for printing
such number as shall be thought proper
Order'd
That he get 1000 printed forthwith,
And that the Court be movd to empower the Comittee
of Treasury to affix the Companys Seal to 50 of them
numbred from 1 to 50 inclusive to accompany the
said Letter
Adjourn'd
Here ends the Proceedings of the
Comittee of Correspondence appointed
The 9th. of February 1732/3
