Tuesday 3d June 1735
At a Comittee of Correspondence
Present
The Sub Governor
The Deputy Govr.
Sir John Lade Dr Wood
Mr Edwards Mr Muilman
The Secretary laid before the Comittee a letter of the 26th past
which he had receive from Sir Thomas Geraldino touching
the sorm of the Bill of Sale to be issued by the Companys
Factors at Havana and St Jago de Cuba, and the Passes for
Ships carrying Negroes thither, and desiring Copys of
the orders given to those Factorys relating to the Interventor
And the said Letter being read
The Comittee also read and Considered the Bill of Sale
and the directions given concerning it, And are of Opinion
That the same are very reasonable and proper
Also read the lass and are of Opinion there is no reason
for objecting to the Form thereof
Orderd That the said Bill of Sale be laid before
the Court
Order'd
That the Secretary give Sir Thos Geraldino
Copys of the orders to the said Factorys concerning the
Interventor at each place
Read in Court 6th do
And agreed to
Adjourn'd.
