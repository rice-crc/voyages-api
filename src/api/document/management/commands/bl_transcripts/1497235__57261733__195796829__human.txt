Certificate of their not having been in Jamaica above four Month

any opposition should be made on account of the Negroes not
coming directly from Africa, which We do not apprehend there
will) you are to Insist on, And upon the Vessels arrival you
are to take care that the Master Complys with the 21st Article of
the Assiento Contract in Certifying the health of the Negroes, wch
is also to be observed in futures, if you find that has been the Custom
7. Being got to Carthagena you are forthwith to
Signify your arrival to the Governor, and to wait on him with
our Letter herewith delivered you and make him our Compliments
presenting to him at the same time your Power under the Companys
Seal (which is in the Usual Form) And We hope he will without
any Scruple or Embarrasment admit you as the Comp Factors
and Register your powers accordingly, if necessary
You are in the like manner to wait on the Roy Officers
and make them our Compliments delivering to them our Letter also
herewith delivered you
And inasmuch as it is highty necessary for the
better facilitating the Companys Affairs under your Management
that a good understanding and Harmony bekept up between your
Selves and the Governors and other Spanish Officers in Authority
We recomend it to you to promote the same on all occasions
