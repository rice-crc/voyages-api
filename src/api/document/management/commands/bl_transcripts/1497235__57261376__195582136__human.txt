You are to be very Circumspect in your behaviour an
the Spariards consucting your self in a sober queet peaceable orderlo
becoming manner and to carry yourself with a suitable & due rear
& Durrum towards the Factors, under whose immediate Direction
are, and Wedoubt not of their treating you with Civility & kindnes
You are to avoid all manner of Gaming and to be
very Cautious not to give offence to therse of the Reman Cathetick Religion
it being forbrd under severe penaltys by the Assiento Contract
neither are you to Marry any of the Natives or Inhabitants of the
Spanish Dominions or profess the Roman Catholick Religion in
regard of the many Inconveniencys that may arise therefrom to
mpany Conuerns
We ard no more here but recomend it to you as
your own promotion depends upon it to be Sober Diligent Careful
and Industrious in the dispatch of the Business of the Tactory
which will be the only means of recomending your self to Our
Favour and Encouragement
Towishing yoru a good doyage Hereman
S. Sea House London
11 March 1728/9
Your Loving Friends
