Instructions gunby theboust of Directors
of the South Sea Company to Mr Peter Sergeant
appointed one of the Writers to the Panama &
Portobelo Factory
Having choosen Messrs Richard Rigby Henry Johnson
and James Echlin to be our Factors at Panama and Portovelo for the
managing and carrying on the Companys Affairs in those parts relating
to the Assunts, and reposing a special Trust and confidence in your
Integrity videlity and deligent application to business, We have
approved of you as second Writer to the said Factory the other Writer
being Mr Moses Davison) and you Leaving entred into such Bond
with Sureties as We have required for the regular and faithful
discharge of your Duty You are to proceed with our aforesaid Factors
to Portobels by way of Jamaica, and they are to bear ye necessary
Expences of your Voyage and during your stay in Jamaia
Your Business as Writer will be to Copy Letters Accounts
and other Papers and to do such Writing and other Business pertaining
to the Factory, as they shall set you upon or direct
You are to take Care not to divielge shew or produce
any the Books Letters papers or Accounts of or belonging to the Factory
to any Personor persons except the Factors, but cautiously & carefully
to preferve and keep them
Your Salary is to be after the Rate of 500 Ps 8/8 P Ann
and the same as well as the Expences of your Lodging and icting during
YourContinuance in that Station are to be paid & born by the Factory
And for your Encouragement to be have well in the
Service of the Factory, We have instructed our Factors that in Case the
present first Writer should either comence Book keeper or dye, then you
as second Writer are to take his places and comence first Writer
And afterwards upon the decease of the Book keeper, you are also to take
place as such with the advantage of Succession attending the same
on the decease of any the Factors, untill and unless the Court of Director
for the time being shall signify their pleasure to the contrary which
We hope you will not by your Conduct give them occasion to do
