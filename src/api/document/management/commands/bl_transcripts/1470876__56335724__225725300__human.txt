Present Present

Sr. Thos Goraldins Sr. Thos Geraldino
Sir John Lade Sir John Lade
The Comittee made some progress in reading the Letters
lately receivd from Vera Cruz and agreed to proceed further
at next Meeting
Mr Blackwood attending was call'd in and deliverd
to the Comittee three Certificates in Spanish of his Catholick
Majestys Officers at Campeche Testifying that the Passes
which were granted by the Company Numbred A B & C.
for the Ships Derby St Quintin and Eagle were detained
by them the said Spanish Officers and filed in the
Contaduria, and Certifying also the Measurement of the
Negroes by the said Ships
Read & agreed to 
10th do 
Adjourn'd
