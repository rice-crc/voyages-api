The Comittee having taken into Consideration the Letter
from his Exrs Mr Keene of the 28th Ultimo, and the papers
which accompanied the same refen'd by the Court yesterday
Are of opinion a Letter beprepared by the Secretary
returning his Exy Thanks for his Care & Zeal in defending
the Companys Right in relation to the value of the Collars
payable to the King of Spain for the Negro Duty, and
referring his Excy to such further orders & Instructions
as he shall receive from his Grace the Duke of Newcastle
in relation thereunto
Order'd
That Copys of the said Letters and
Papers be imediately made and transmitted to the Duke
of Newcastle requesting his Grace to obtain his Majestys
Orders as soon as conveniently may be to his Excellency
Mr Keone on this Affair, which is of so great Importance
to the Company
