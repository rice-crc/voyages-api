To the Kings most Excellent Majesty
Most Gracious Sovereign.
Your Majesty mast Ductiful & Loyal
Subjects the South seabompany in General Court assembled
humbly beg leave to Represent, That having ever since your
Majestys happy accession to the Throne of these Kingdoms,
had the Honoer of Your Majesty for their Governor
They most humbly Beseech Your Majesty
That they may be still under Your Royal
Patronage, and that you will be graciously
pleased by continuing their Governor, to grant
Thom so Auspicious a Mark of your Favour
By order of the Geonenal Cart
P. Burrell Sub Govr
J Bristow Depy Gor
London
17 January 1744
