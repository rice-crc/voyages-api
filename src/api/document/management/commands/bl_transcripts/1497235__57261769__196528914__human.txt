23. And whereas it has always been customary after a
Suspension of the Assiento, occasioned by Ruptures between the two
Crowns, to open an Indulto on Negroes illicitly Introduced, for such
a limited time and at such price as shall be thought reasonable, wch
Indulgence may have a better effect by inducing the people to manifest
their Negroes, than if We proceeded by way of Seizures, We do therefore
direct you for once to open an Indulto at Carthagena for such time &
at such price as you yourselves shall judge reasonable wth Indulto
is to be set on foot as soon as possible after your Arrival at your
Factory, to which and you are to apply without loss of time to the
Governor or other persons whom it may Concerm
After the time fixed for such Indulto shall be expired
you are not to presume to open any other Indulto without the special
leave of the Court of Directors previously obtained, but to use your
utmost Endeavours with the Assistance of the Governor and Royal
Officers to Seize and Confiscate all Negroes ellicitly Introduced, and
which shall not have been Indulted by you pursuant to the power
granted by the Assiento Contract, and as an encouragement for your so
doing We have agreed to allow you one half of the Net produce of all
Negroes so Seized and Confiscated to be divided, One third of such
Moiety to the particular Factor or Factors or other person in the
Companys service who shall be present at the making the Seizures
and the other two thirds thereof among the Factors in general
according to their Shares in Comission
