Instructions given by the Court of Directors
of the South Sea Company to Mr Peter D'Oyle appointed
one of the Surgeons to the Panama & Porto belo Tactory
Having appointed Messrs Richard Rigby & Henry Johnson and James Echlin
to be our Factors for Panama and Portobelo for the managing & carrying
on the Companys Affairs in those parts relating to the Assiento; And
having entertaind a good opinion of your Skill and understanding in
Physick and Surgery, and of your Integrity and diligent application to
Business We have approved of you as one of the Surgeons to the said Factory
(the other Surgeon being Mr John Innes) and you having entred into such
Bonds with Sureties as We have required for the regular and faithful
discharge of your Decty You are to proceed with our aforesaid Factors to
Portobelo by the way of Jamaica, and they are to bear the necessary Expence
of your Voyage and during your stay in Jamaica
Your particular Business as Surgeon will be to
Administer Physick and Surgery to the Factory as well as Negroes
out of such Medicines as shall from time to time be provided for you by
the Factory for the time being, and daily to visit the Negroes taking
particular Care of the Sick, and prefering and looking after them
in the best manner you can during the time of their Sickness keeping
a Journal or Diary of your Practise, which you are to lay before
the Factors when thereto required, and to transmit to Us Copys thereof
from time to time
You are to lay before our Factors a Weekly or Monthly
Account, as they shall direct of the State and Condition of ye Negroes
and how many have died in that Time, and of what distempers, &
to transmit us Copys thereof from Time to Time
You are also from time to time to lay before the Factors
Indents of such Medicines Utensils and Materials for the Use
of the Negroes as you shall be in want of, That they may have
Timely notice for supplying You
Your Salary (which is to be after the rate of 666 Ps 8/8
and 2/3 of a piece of Eight P Annum) as well as the Exspences of your Lodging
and Dyet during your Continuance in the Factory are to be paid and born
by them
