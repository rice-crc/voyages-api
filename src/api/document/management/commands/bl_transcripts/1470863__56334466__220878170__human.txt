Tuesday 6th April 1730
At a Comittee for Correspondence and Factorys
Present
The Dep: Govr Mr Rigby
Sr. John Lock Mr Roberts
The Comittee proceeded on the remainder of the Dispatches for
the Buenos Ayres Factory and finishd the same Viz
Instructions to Mr Humphreys the Bookkeeper
Mr Young the Surgeons Instructions and Contract
Bond for the Comander of the Store Ship to Execute with
Suretys in the Penalty of £2000
A Letter to Mr Cross and the other Factors now at Bs Ayres
Letters to the Ger and Royal Officers of Buenos Ayres
Orderd/
That all the Dispatches for the Bs. Ayres Factory
be laid before the Court
read in Court
10th Do WS 
Adjournd
