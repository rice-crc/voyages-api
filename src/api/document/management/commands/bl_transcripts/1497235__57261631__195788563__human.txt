17. You are from Time to time to send us Authentick
Certficates of the Measurement of each Corge of Negroes that We
may know how many pieces of India they were regulated at, &
thereby be enabled to Adjust the Dutys We are to pay at Madrid
to his Catholick Majesty, And you are not to omit to Advise Us
the day of each Cargoes Arrival their Condition, and whether any
and what Negroes died in the Voyage, or within fifteen days after
Landing For in this last case We are not liable to pay Duties
for such Negroes But as We pay dutys for the whole Number
at Madrid You must not omit to transmit us Authontick
Certificates of such as dye in the 15t days that We may deduct
the Dutys from them out of the general Negro dutys
18. You are to endeavour to get as many Negroes allow'd
for a piece of India as you can, but not to give any Fees to obtain it
19. Youare to be very careful not to do or suffer to be done
any manner of thing repugnant to the Assiento Contract, or the
Subsequent Convention of the Year 1716
20. You are to provide your selves with the necessary
Beams Scales and Weights, which you are to make use of in
Weighing the Gold Silver Bullion and ps to you receive, And
We recomend it to you to be very cautions what Gold & Silver
you take there being great decit used therein, especially in dust
Gold, and you are on no pretence whatsoever to take Virgin Silver
or Gold enstampd, or that has not paid the King of Spains Luinto
the same being forbid by the 10 & 25 Articles of the Assiento
