Coppy of a Journall up The River Gambia, found
amongst Dr:Hooks papers.
Your importunity together with my gratitude to you for your
 most curious imformations & instructions in the mechanicks (without 
which I can ? my labour had been in vain) has escorted that from me,
which I confess the resolution I had a new taken to the contrary by 
resolving never to  divluge either for love or force. To which end I expect
according to that Faithfull & Solemn promise of Secrecy both of the
business it self, & likewise of which I would not sould be known to 
the King for 10.000
