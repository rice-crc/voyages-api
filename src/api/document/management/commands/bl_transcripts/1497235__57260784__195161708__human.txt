Gentlemen
It is with great pleasure We acquaint you that the mis
understandings between the two Crowns are at length torminated and
Peace happily Concluded
Our Satisfaction is not only That We have a Seace with
Spain with whom We are always glad to live in perfect Friendship and
Harmony, But in regard that the Assients is again renewd and
Confirmed to Us, whereby We have this Opportunity of assuring you
of our Constant Regard, and that We shall be glad to have it in our
power to do you any acceptable Services
The Gentlemen who will have the pleasure to deliver ye
this are Richd Righy Esqr who We have authorized and appointed to be
president or Chief of our Panama and Portobelo Factory, and Mr Henry
Johnson who We have also appointed to be our other Factor We desire
Gentlemen, your Favour and Freendship to them and the Royel
Assien to Affairs undertheir Conduct and Care, which We trust the
will not do any thing to forfeet their Claim to
We pray God to preserve you many years, and remain
London
6 Sept 1723
Gentlemen
Your affectionate Friends
the Court of Directors of the
Royal Assiento Company
