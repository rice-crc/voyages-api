South Sea house 13th Decr 1716
Sr.
The South Sea Co having some Fustick on board the
Bedford, which I have wrote to Captn Liele to deliver to you I /(by
comand of the Court of Directors send you inclosed the Bill of Lading
for the same, and desire you to send it up to London by the first
good Conveyance by Sea on the best terms you can and P hope
you will so manage your Treaty with the owners, or matter of
the ship or Vessel that shall be agreed with to bring it round that it
may be delivered on board from the Bedfords side without the
Charge of Landing it being of small value, and that you will
(for the Information of the Court) give me an account with
whom and on what Terme you agree and of all yor Roceding
in this Affair. I am
Sr
Yor most humble Servt
Jno Ury
