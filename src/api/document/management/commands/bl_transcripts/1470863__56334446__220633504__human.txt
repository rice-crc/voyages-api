Thursday 2d Octor: 1729
At a Joynt Comittee for Correspondence and Factorys
Present
The Dep. Gov.
Dr Wood
Capt. Jones
Mr Brooke
The Comittee made a Progress in settling the Draft of the
Indenture of Covenants between the Company and the Buenos Ayres
Factory, and intend to proceed further at next meeting
Adjournd
Thursday 9th Octor 1729
At a Comittee for Correspondence and Factorys
Present
The Sub: Govr The Dep: Govr
Sr. Rd. Hopkins Mr Burrell
Capt. Jones. Mr Roberts
The Comittee agreed to a posseript to the Instructions
to the Havana Factory, to Accompany Sundry Books Accounts
