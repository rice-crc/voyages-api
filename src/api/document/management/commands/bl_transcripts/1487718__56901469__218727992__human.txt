Thursday 27th June 1728
At a Joynt Comittee of Corespondence & Factorys
Present
The Sub Govr
The Dep. Geo:
Mr Ashton
Mr Halsey
Mr Brooke
Mr Roberts
Mr Bowles
Mr Burrell
The Comittee took into Consideration the Order of Court of the
2s Instant referring it to them to Consider what is propee to be dene
with regard to the Companys Effects and Concerns at Panama and
Portobelo, and are of Opinion that Mr Richard Rigby now in London
and Mr Honry Johnson be recomended to the Court to be sent over under proper
Instructions for taking care of the Companys Effects and Concerns
in these parts.
Read in Court
28th do vide Mms WS
Adjournd
Wednesday 3 July 1728
At a Joynt Comittee of Correspondence & Factorys
Present
The Sub Govr The Dept Gov:
Sr Rd Hophins Mr Roberts
Mr Burrell Mr Brooke
The Comittee proceeded on the Examination of Mr Johnsons Conduct
whilst in the Companys Service at Panama and Portobelo, and read
over the Charges against him as extracted out of the Letters and Papers, on
Mr Johnsons Answer & intend to proceed further at their Next meeting
Adjournd
