The Comittee read the Letters from the Carthagena Factory of
23d & 28th April last, and directed the Secretary to prepare an
Answer to them
The said Factory having sent the Form of the Bill of
Sale for Negroes which was issued by their predecessors, and
advised that the same is most Convenient, therefore desiring
a parcel in that Form
Order'd
That 2000 be forthwith Printed
The said Factory having with their frst mention'd Letter
Ttransmitted an Authentick Testimony of the Indulto opend
in the years 1729& 1730 at St Martha by the late Governor
there Don Joseph de Andia, whereby it appears there is due
to the Company from His Catholick Majesty & 13049:6
Clear of all Charges,
Orderd That the said Testimony be carefully presend
And that the Accompt place the said Sum to his Cm Account
Order'd That Extract of the said Letter of 23d April,
and Copy of the other of the 28 do touching the Behaviour
of the Governor and Royal Officers at Carthagena be sent to
Mr Keene, and that the like be given to St Thos Geraldino
Read in Court 23 do
And agreed to
Adjourn'd
